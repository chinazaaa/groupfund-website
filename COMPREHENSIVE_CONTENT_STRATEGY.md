## üé• VIDEO CONTENT (High Priority)

### Product Demo Videos
1. **2-Minute Product Overview**
   - What GroupFund does
   - Key features showcase
   - Call-to-action to join waitlist
   - Use for: Website homepage, social media, ads

2. **5-Minute Feature Walkthrough**
   - Step-by-step: Creating a group
   - Setting up contributions
   - Tracking payments
   - Member reliability scores
   - Use for: YouTube, website, email campaigns

3. **30-Second Social Media Videos**
   - Quick problem-solution format
   - "Before vs After" comparisons
   - Feature highlights
   - Use for: Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook Stories

4. **Use Case Videos** (1-2 minutes each)
   - "How Families Use GroupFund"
   - "How Churches Use GroupFund"
   - "How Friend Groups Use GroupFund"
   - "How Offices Use GroupFund"

5. **Testimonial Videos**
   - Real users sharing their stories
   - Before/after scenarios
   - Specific results and benefits
   - Use for: Website, social proof, ads

### Educational Video Series
6. **"GroupFund Academy" Series**
   - "Setting Up Your First Group" (5 min)
   - "Managing Large Groups" (5 min)
   - "Handling Late Payments" (5 min)
   - "Multi-Currency Groups" (5 min)
   - Post on YouTube, embed on website

7. **Live Q&A Sessions**
   - Weekly/monthly live sessions
   - Answer user questions
   - Feature updates
   - Build community

### Video Scripts Needed
- Product demo script
- Feature highlight scripts
- Testimonial interview questions
- Educational video outlines

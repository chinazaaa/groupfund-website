## METRICS TO TRACK

After the first week, track:
- **Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares per post
- **Reach:** How many people saw each post
- **Clicks:** How many clicked your waitlist link
- **Conversions:** How many signed up for waitlist
- **Best Performing:** Which post type got most engagement
- **Best Time:** What time got most engagement
